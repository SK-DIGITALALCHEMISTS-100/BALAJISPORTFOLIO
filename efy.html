Expiryfy

ExpiryFY is a smart shelf life and product expiry alert system developed to help retailers, wholesalers, and supermarkets efficiently track inventory and reduce product wastage. Traditional manual checking of expiry dates is time-consuming, error-prone, and often results in financial loss due to expired goods. ExpiryFY addresses these challenges by providing an automated, real-time solution for monitoring product shelf life with timely notifications.

  • Client: Atchaya T
  • Status: complete

ExpiryFY enables businesses to record product details including manufacturing and expiry dates, and automatically tracks their validity. The system sends alerts three days before expiry via email or dashboard notifications, ensuring that retailers can act on time by offering discounts, moving stock, or removing expired items from shelves.

The platform supports multiple roles including administrators, employees, and managers, each with role-based dashboards. Administrators can manage racks, products, suppliers, and notifications, while staff can update stock details and view upcoming expiry alerts. Reports and analytics are also available to provide insights into product turnover, wastage reduction, and sales optimization.

ExpiryFY significantly reduces losses from expired products, improves operational efficiency, and promotes customer trust by ensuring that only fresh and valid items are sold. With integration of barcode/QR scanning, storage management, and automated email alerts, it creates a reliable and transparent system for inventory control.
