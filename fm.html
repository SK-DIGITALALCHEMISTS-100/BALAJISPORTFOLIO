Fresh Mart

Fresh Mart is a web-based application developed for a juice and fruit shop that offers fresh juices, dates, and seasonal fruits directly to customers. The platform aims to bring healthy, hygienic beverages and fresh produce online.

  • Client: Bhuvana
  • Completed: 25 Sep 2024

Fresh Mart is a web-based application developed for a juice and fruit shop that offers fresh juices, dates, and seasonal fruits directly to customers. The platform aims to bring healthy, hygienic beverages and fresh produce online, providing users with an easy, contactless shopping experience. It is ideal for health-conscious customers who prefer freshly made drinks and naturally sourced fruits delivered to their doorstep.

Customers can register on the platform, browse various categories like fresh juices (e.g., orange, pomegranate), dates (Ajwa, Medjool), and fruits (mangoes, apples, bananas), add items to the cart, and place orders with secure checkout. The application is mobile-friendly, allowing users to order anytime, anywhere. Sellers (juice shop admin) can manage product listings, prices, stock availability, and order tracking through a dedicated admin dashboard.

Technologies Used: The platform is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap for the front end, with Python Django as the backend and SQLite as the database. It supports user authentication, product filtering, admin control, and optional coupon systems.

Fresh Mart not only promotes healthy lifestyle choices but also empowers local juice shops to expand their reach through digital transformation.
