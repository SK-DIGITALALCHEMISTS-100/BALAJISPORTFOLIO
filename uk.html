Uzhavankart

Uzhavan Kart is a farmer-to-customer e-commerce platform designed to empower farmers and provide consumers with fresh, chemical-free produce directly from the source. Traditional supply chains often involve multiple intermediaries, leading to higher costs, delayed delivery, and reduced profits for farmers. Uzhavan Kart solves these issues by creating a transparent, subscription-based system that connects farmers and consumers seamlessly.

  • Project: MCA final sem project
  • Status: Complete
  • Deployed: Customer Page

Uzhavan Kart enables consumers to subscribe for daily, weekly, or monthly fresh produce delivered directly to their doorstep. With storage hubs established in every taluk and a 20-minute delivery radius, it ensures freshness and reliability. Farmers benefit from fair pricing, reduced wastage, and access to a larger customer base, while consumers enjoy healthier, farm-fresh food at affordable rates.

The platform supports multiple roles including administrators, farmers, delivery staff, and customers, each with a customized dashboard. Administrators can manage hubs, logistics, employees, and product pricing, while farmers can upload produce details and track earnings. Customers can browse products, manage subscriptions, and monitor deliveries through an easy-to-use mobile/web application.

Uzhavan Kart minimizes post-harvest losses, ensures timely delivery, and eliminates middlemen, thereby enhancing farmers’ income and customer trust. With features like online payments, real-time delivery tracking, and customer feedback integration, it delivers a modern, reliable, and scalable solution for agri-commerce.

By bridging the gap between farmers and customers, Uzhavan Kart not only promotes sustainable farming but also strengthens the rural economy. It stands as a step towards building a healthier, transparent, and farmer-friendly marketplace.
