Spy Camera Detector is a security-focused project designed to detect hidden surveillance devices such as miniature or concealed cameras in sensitive areas like changing rooms, hotel rooms, restrooms, or conference halls. With the rise in privacy breaches due to unauthorized surveillance, there is an increasing need for portable, cost-effective, and efficient detection systems. This project aims to develop a user-friendly device or application that helps individuals identify hidden spy cameras and protect their personal privacy.

The system functions by leveraging technologies such as infrared (IR) reflection, radio frequency (RF) signal detection, and lens glare reflection to locate concealed lenses or wireless transmitting devices. When the detector scans an area, it identifies the presence of abnormal light reflection from a hidden camera lens or unusual electromagnetic signals typically emitted by wireless cameras. The device then alerts the user through a buzzer, LED indication, or a mobile app notification.

This project is particularly useful for security personnel, travelers, and individuals concerned about privacy in unfamiliar environments. It offers portability, ease of use, and real-time detection, making it a practical tool for enhancing personal security. Future enhancements may include mobile integration, AI-based visual detection, and automatic scanning features for smarter surveillance protection.
