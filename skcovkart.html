SK CoverKart

SK CoverKart is a comprehensive inventory and sales management system built for a medical covers and masks business. It features a real-time dashboard, stock tracking, customer billing, and invoicing — all within a sleek dark UI.

  • Client: SK CoverKart
  • Category: Inventory Management System
  • Completed: March 2026

Key Features

Dashboard Overview

Real-time stats for total stock, revenue, net profit, and low-stock alerts — all at a glance.

Inventory Control

Add, update and monitor stock levels with activity logs, restock alerts, and product management.

Sales & Billing

Customer cart, billing workflows, and a full invoice history to manage every transaction efficiently.

SK CoverKart is a purpose-built business management system designed for the medical covers and masks industry. It provides business owners with a centralized platform to manage every aspect of their operations — from inventory and stock tracking to customer billing and invoice generation — all within an intuitive, dark-themed interface.

The system's dashboard provides instant insights into key business metrics including total stock items, total revenue, net profit, and low-stock alerts. Recent stock activity is displayed in a live feed, showing IN and OUT transactions with timestamps and quantities. A dedicated "Needs Restock" panel highlights products running critically low, enabling proactive restocking decisions.

On the sales side, SK CoverKart includes a complete customer cart flow, a billing module for generating customer bills, and an all-invoices page for historical record-keeping. Inventory management is fully handled through the Current Stock view, Stock Updates log, Add New Stock, and Add New Product screens. Built with a clean, professional dark UI using local browser storage, SK CoverKart demonstrates full-stack thinking applied to a real-world small business problem — making daily operations faster, more accurate, and paperless.
